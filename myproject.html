Nepal

Nepal is a beautiful country in South Asia, located between India and China. It is famous for the Himalayas, including Mount Everest, the tallest mountain on Earth. The capital city, Kathmandu, is rich in culture, temples, and history. Nepal is known for its diverse landscapes, from high mountains to green hills and plains, as well as its friendly people and unique traditions. Tourism, agriculture, and culture play an important role in the country’s economy and identity.

political status of nepal

Nowadays, the political situation of Nepal has changed significantly after the 2025 youth-led protests against corruption and poor governance, which forced the resignation of the previous government. In the 2026 general election, a new party, the Rastriya Swatantra Party led by Balendra Shah, won a landslide victory and formed the government with a strong majority, reducing the power of traditional parties like Nepali Congress and CPN-UML.
